


Input number of seconds N 
M = N / 60      M-MONTHS-TOTAL
M = MQ , MR     MQ - MONTHS-QUOTIENT , MR- MONTHS-REMAINDER
SS = MR * 60 

H = MQ / 60     H - HOURS-TOTAL 
H = HQ , HR     HQ - HOURS-QUOTIENT , HR- HOURS-REMAINDER
MM = HR * 60

D = HQ / 24     D - DAYS-TOTAL
D = DQ , DR     DQ - DAYS-QUOTIENT , DR- DAYS-REMAINDER
HH = DR * 24 

W = DQ / 7      W - WEEKS-TOTAL
W = WQ , WR     WQ - WEEKS-QUOTIENT , WR- WEEKS-REMAINDER
DD = WR * 7

WW = WQ
